Business information

Our commitment to you

Vela is a subscription software service. Here's what you're agreeing to when you sign up.

What you're paying for

Vela is a monthly or annual software subscription for AI-powered proposal generation, tracking, and revenue analytics. You are charged on a recurring basis at the rate of the plan you select ($49, $99, or $199/month, or annual equivalents).

All charges are processed securely through Stripe. You will see "Vela" or "Vela HQ Inc." on your bank or card statement.

Cancellation & refunds

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your billing dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain full access until then.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, email billing@getvela.com for a full refund, no questions asked.

Delivery of service

Vela is a software-as-a-service product delivered via web browser. Access is granted immediately upon payment confirmation. There is no physical product and no shipping involved.

Service availability is 99.9% uptime target. For planned maintenance, you'll receive at least 24 hours notice by email.

Your data & privacy

Your proposal data, client information, and agency profile are stored securely and never sold to third parties. AI generation uses the Anthropic Claude API — proposal content is not used to train Anthropic's models.

See our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for complete details. Contact us at support@getvela.com with any questions.
